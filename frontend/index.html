Who I Am

Full Stack Developer, Problem Solver & Technology Enthusiast

I'm Vivek Yadav, a full stack developer based in Lucknow who enjoys turning ambiguous ideas into applications people actually want to use. My journey started with curiosity about how websites work, and it hasn't slowed down since — I'm a fast learner who picks up new frameworks and tools quickly, and a genuine technology enthusiast who reads changelogs for fun.

What drives me is problem solving: breaking a messy requirement down into a clean architecture, then shipping it with code that's easy to read and easy to maintain. I care about the details — performance, accessibility, and the small interactions that make a product feel polished — as much as I care about the big picture.

Whether it's a finance tracker crunching real numbers or a platform connecting workers with employers, I approach every project as a curious, passionate developer who's always looking for the next thing to learn.
